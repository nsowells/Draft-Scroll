2017 Class Highlight
The Greatest Day 3 Draft in NFL History
The 2017 class produced Kittle at #146, Aaron Jones at #182, Quandre Diggs at #165, Matt Milano at #163, Desmond King at #151, and Harrison Butker at #233. Round 5 alone generated more Pro Bowl appearances than most entire draft classes. And at #69 in Round 3, an FCS receiver from Eastern Washington posted the greatest single receiving season in NFL history.
